Pokémon Regions

What are the regions?


There is a total of 8 regions in the current Pokémon franchise. Each of the eight generations focuses on a new region. In every new generation, there will be a whole new set of unique Pokémons, Moves, Abilities & Types. Given that, there will be 3 unique Pokémon starters of each region.
However, in Jul's Pokémon Information Centre, the eighth generation (Sword and Shield) data might be inaccurate and lacking in various aspects due to the fact that it is not taken directly from Nintendo's Pokemon ROMs.
