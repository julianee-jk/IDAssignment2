Pokémon Regions

What are the regions?


There is a total of 8 regions in the current Pokémon franchise. Each of the eight generations focuses on a new region. In every new generation, there will be a whole new set of unique Pokémons. Given that, there will be 3 unique Pokémon starters of each region.
However, in Jul's Pokémon Information Centre, the eighth generation is currently being added as it is still updating with new additions.
